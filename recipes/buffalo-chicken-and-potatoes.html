Buffalo Chicken and Potatoes

Description

A high protein, low calorie buffalo chicken potato bowl for meal prepping.

Ingredients

  • 750g Raw Chicken Breast
  • Raw White Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cumin
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Olive Oil
  • Buffalo Hot Sauce
  • Light Butter

Steps

  1. Cut raw chicken into small cubes
  2. Season with salt, parsley, garlic powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and olive oil
  3. Melt butter in a pan and cook the chicken until golden and charred
  4. Melt more butter and add buffalo hot sauce
  5. Cut potatoes into small cubes
  6. Season with salt, parsley, garlic powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and olive oil
  7. Place the potatoes into the oven for 20 minutes at 395 degrees
